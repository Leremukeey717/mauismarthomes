More than an install

Not just an AV company. Your home's technician for the long run.

Most of our work comes from homeowners we already know — a new zone added to a system we installed years ago, a router swapped out before it becomes a problem, a theater retuned after a remodel.

We keep it simple on purpose: fewer proprietary systems, more standards-based gear that any future technician can service. Your house shouldn't be locked to one company to keep working.

Every project starts with a walk-through of how you actually use the house, not a catalog of what we can sell you.
