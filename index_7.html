Smart Home Technology

What we do

1

TV Installs

From sleek wall-mounted displays to hidden wiring, we create clean entertainment spaces built to perform. Hardwired connections help keep your streaming fast and reliable.

2

Surround Sound

Watch movies how they were intended. Listen to dialogue clearly, hear the plane fly overhead, hear the rumble of explosions. Experience the music of the soundtrack.

3

Home Audio

Music that follows you throughout your home. From cooking and BBQs to relaxing after a long day, enjoy seamless audio in every space with an audio system for your daily life.

4

Surveillance

Most homes want a video doorbell and surveillance cameras. See who comes to your home and what happens on the street in front of your house. Whether it's a fun prank, a surprise engagement, or a police incident, make sure it's recorded.

5

Wifi

Homes need strong internet connections for video calls with family, streaming movies, and running a home business. We make sure all your devices have a strong connection.

6

Shades

Automate your shades to rise and lower with the sun. You don't need to walk around to every window — let technology make your life easier.

7

Accent Lighting

Bathroom toe-kick lighting, focused task lighting in the kitchen or workshop. A few accent lights will elevate your home.

8

Home Automation

Turn off everything when you leave. Control your garage door, locks, climate control, even vacuums from your phone or on a schedule.
